Important HTML Tags

<h1> - <h6>
Defines HTML headings, where <h1> is the largest and <h6> is the smallest.
<p>
Defines a paragraph.
<img>
Embeds an image in the webpage.
<a>
Defines a hyperlink to another webpage or resource.
<ul> & <li>
Creates an unordered list with list items.
